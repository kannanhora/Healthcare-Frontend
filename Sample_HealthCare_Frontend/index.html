The Problems with Current Cancer Treatments

Lack of Personalized Care

Most cancer programs follow a generic approach, neglecting personalized treatment plans. Every patient’s journey is different, but standardized protocols often overlook individual needs, leading to suboptimal recovery.

Side Effects and Quality of Life

Chemotherapy can cause temporary side effects like hair loss, nausea, and fatigue. Some cancer treatments may lead to long-term health challenges for survivors.

Slow Adoption of Technology

Despite advancements in AI and data-driven healthcare, many cancer programs still rely on outdated methods. This leads to inefficiencies in diagnosis, treatment planning, and patient monitoring.
